A Little About Me

My name is Nurjahan Jhorna. Nur meaning light and jahan meaning world, together it means light of the world and Jhorna means waterfall. I am currently a sophomore college student at New Jersey Institute of Technology, and I am majoring in Web & Information Systems. This is my first website and I had a lot of fun and frustrations making it but in the end it was worth it. I wanted to make a personal website that contained everything that helps me with anxiety. I wanted to make this website as a recommendation for people who struggle with anxiety same as me and hope that everything helps me helps them a little bit.
